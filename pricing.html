FAQ

Can I start with Basic and upgrade later?

Yes. Many founders begin with strategic assessment first, then move into broader support once the business direction is clearer.

Does Standard include the visa filing stage?

No. Standard focuses on the business-plan and endorsement-preparation side. Visa filing assistance is part of Premium.

What if I do not yet have a strong business idea?

Basic is usually the right entry point because it gives you a structured view of whether the idea can be improved into a viable case.

Delivery expectations

Client participation is required

The strongest output always depends on timely founder input, honest background information, and active collaboration during drafting and review.

Timelines vary by readiness

A founder with documents, market clarity, and English evidence ready moves faster than someone still building the case from scratch.

Revisions depend on scope

Minor refinements are normal. Major strategy changes can require additional work or a package upgrade.

Next step

Choose the package that matches the founder's actual stage.
