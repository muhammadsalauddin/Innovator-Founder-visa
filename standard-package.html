Detailed scope

What the Standard package covers

  • Everything included in Basic, including strategic assessment and business idea review.
  • Business plan support that sharpens the commercial case and founder narrative.
  • Pitch deck development aligned with how the venture should be presented clearly and professionally.
  • Structured endorsement application guidance and document packaging support.
  • Review support on the core documents most likely to shape endorsement credibility.

Best fit

Choose Standard if

Useful when

The founder is serious about the route but still lacks a polished business plan, deck, or structured case narrative.

Expected result

The founder ends with stronger materials, better market logic, and improved endorsement readiness.

Founder preparation

What the founder should have ready

  • A defined or partially defined business idea with enough substance to work from.
  • Founder background, CV, and relevant business or sector experience.
  • Any market notes, draft materials, early traction, research, or concept evidence.
  • Readiness to participate actively in revisions and clarification rounds.

Not included

What Standard does not cover

  • Full visa application filing assistance.
  • Post-approval company setup execution.
  • Post-visa growth and ILR planning support.
  • Government fees, test fees, or third-party service costs.
