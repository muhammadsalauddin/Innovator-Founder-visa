Detailed scope

What the Basic package covers

  • Assessment of founder background, business direction, and route suitability.
  • Strategy call focused on the strongest route, founder position, and commercial weaknesses.
  • Review of the current business idea against endorsement expectations.
  • Clear indication of what needs to be improved before moving into business-plan development.
  • High-level action roadmap so the founder knows what to prepare next.

Best fit

Choose Basic if

The founder still needs clarity on whether the idea is suitable and what the route actually requires.

Useful when

The case is early, the idea is rough, or the founder wants an expert-first assessment before paying for broader support.

Expected result

Clarity on readiness, a stronger understanding of the route, and a decision on whether to stop, refine, or upgrade.

Founder preparation

What the founder should have ready

  • A short explanation of the business idea, even if incomplete.
  • Basic founder background, work experience, and any previous business exposure.
  • Any documents or notes that help explain the concept, target market, or personal goals.
  • Honest visibility on weak areas such as missing English proof, unclear finances, or weak innovation logic.

Not included

What Basic does not cover

  • Full business plan drafting.
  • Pitch deck development.
  • Full endorsement application packaging and submission support.
  • Visa filing assistance or post-approval company setup guidance.

FAQ

Is Basic enough if the founder has no clear idea yet?

Yes. That is one of the strongest use cases for Basic because it helps decide whether the concept can be improved into a credible route.

Can the founder upgrade after this stage?

Yes. If the business looks viable, the normal next step is moving into Standard for plan and deck development.

Does Basic include endorsement submission?

No. It provides the strategy and readiness view, not the full build and submission process.
