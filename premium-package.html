Detailed scope

What the Premium package covers

  • Everything included in Standard, including business plan work, deck development, and endorsement preparation.
  • End-to-end endorsement support through the final preparation stage.
  • Visa application assistance and evidence coordination aligned with the business case.
  • UK company setup guidance after approval so the founder can move into execution more cleanly.
  • Post-visa growth and ILR planning support at a strategic level.

Founder preparation

What the founder should have ready

  • Core founder documents, business concept, and active participation in drafting and review.
  • English and financial requirement planning so the visa stage is not delayed later.
  • Readiness to move from endorsement preparation into visa coordination and early launch planning.
  • Clear communication on timing, move plans, family considerations, and post-approval goals.

Important limits

What Premium still does not mean

  • No guaranteed endorsement or visa approval.
  • No government fees, legal filing fees, or third-party external costs included by default.
  • No substitute for founder participation, document accuracy, or honest disclosure.
  • Company setup guidance does not automatically mean outsourced operational execution of every launch task.
