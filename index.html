FAQ

Questions founders ask before starting.

Do I need investment? +

Not necessarily. What matters more is whether your business is innovative, viable, and scalable, and whether you can present it credibly.

How long does it take? +

Timelines vary by readiness, endorsement body review, and document preparation. We map a realistic timeline after the first consultation.

What if I don't have a business idea? +

That is common. We can help you build, refine, and validate a concept based on your background and market opportunities.

Can you guarantee approval? +

No serious advisor should guarantee approval. What we do guarantee is a rigorous, founder-focused process designed to maximize the quality of your case.
