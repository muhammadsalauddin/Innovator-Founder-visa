Phases A-D

Foundation

A. Consultation

We start by understanding the founder, the business idea, and the realistic route fit.

Success marker

B. Eligibility review

Success marker

The case is grounded in real requirements, not assumptions.

C-D. Idea validation and market positioning

We test whether the idea can stand up commercially in the UK and refine its positioning.

Success marker

Phases E-H

Build the Case

E. Business model build

Success marker

Success marker

Approval to Growth

K. Relocation and launch

Success marker

The founder is building a sustainable UK business, not just completing a one-off application.

What drives success

  • A business idea that is genuinely stronger than a generic trading concept.
  • A founder who understands the market and can explain the business clearly.
  • Consistent documentation across business, endorsement, and visa stages.
  • Preparation for what happens after approval, not just focus on the application itself.

Common blockers

  • Weak business ideas that do not show innovation or scale.
  • Founders who cannot explain the model confidently.
  • Late discovery of English, finance, or immigration-history issues.
  • Template-style plans that lack commercial credibility.
